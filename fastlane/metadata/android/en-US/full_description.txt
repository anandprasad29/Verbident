Verbident helps children prepare for dental visits through visual storytelling and interactive exploration.

<b>FEATURES:</b>

📖 <b>Before the Visit Story</b>
Walk through a dental visit step-by-step with friendly illustrations. Children can see what to expect: the dentist chair, dental mirror, bright light, and more.

🔧 <b>Dental Tools Library</b>
Explore common dental tools and equipment in a safe, non-threatening way. Tap any item to hear its name spoken aloud.

🔊 <b>Text-to-Speech</b>
Every image can be tapped to hear its description, helping children learn vocabulary and feel comfortable with dental terminology.

👨‍👩‍👧 <b>Perfect For:</b>
• Children visiting the dentist for the first time
• Kids with dental anxiety
• Children with autism or sensory sensitivities
• Parents who want to prepare their children

🔒 <b>Privacy First</b>
No accounts required. No data collection. Everything stays on your device.

🌍 <b>Multilingual Support</b>
Available in English and Spanish.
